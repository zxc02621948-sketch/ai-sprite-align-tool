How to use:
1. Drop in a spritesheet.
2. Set columns and rows, e.g. 4x2.
3. If the effect is cut by grid lines, raise "Sampling bleed".
4. Click "Analyze cells" to see each cell’s subject box.
5. If the rows are slash-into-explosion, turn on "Shared impact point".
6. Click "Auto-Align".
7. Play to confirm it no longer drifts, then export.

Transparent-background assets work best. Dark-background assets work too, but with lower accuracy.
Open a spritesheet, or just drop an image into the window.
